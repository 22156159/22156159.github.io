Apple

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company that designs, manufactures, and markets consumer electronics, software, and online services. Headquartered in Cupertino, California, it is best known for products like the iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, and Apple Watch, as well as its software platforms (iOS, macOS) and services such as the App Store and Apple Music. Apple is renowned for its integrated hardware and software approach, which is central to its business model and reputation for user-friendly, well-designed products.
