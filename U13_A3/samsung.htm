Samsung

Samsung is a South Korean multinational conglomerate with a history dating back to 1938, though the well-known Samsung Electronics was founded in 1969. It began as a trading company and has since grown into a global leader in consumer electronics, information technology, and device solutions. Samsung is known for producing a wide range of products like TVs, smartphones, and appliances, and operates with a philosophy of creating superior products and contributing to society.
