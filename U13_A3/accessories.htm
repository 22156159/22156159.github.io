Accessories

Mobile phone accessories cover a variety of items developed to improve the functionality, protection and personal touch of mobile devices. Accessories range from cases and screen protectors that protect devices from damage, to chargers and power banks that keep devices charged through the day. Audio-related accessories, including headphones and speakers improve the listening experience; there are car mounts and holders that make it easier to use the device in cars. There are sufficient variety in styles and features of mobile phone accessories to accommodate and satisfy varied user needs and preferences. It is difficult to imagine a modern smartphone user not relying on at least one or more mobile accessories.

  • The AirPods Pro 3 feature built-in heart rate sensing for workouts, a more secure and comfortable design with foam-infused ear tips and five size options, and up to 4x more Active Noise Cancellation than the original AirPods Pro. They also introduce Live Translation powered by Apple Intelligence, enhance battery life, and offer advanced features like Hearing Test, Hearing Aid, and Hearing Protection.

  • The Samsung Galaxy Buds 3 Pro are true wireless earbuds featuring a redesigned, comfortable fit with "blade lights" on the stems, and a new, transparent-covered charging case. They provide high-fidelity audio via the Samsung Seamless Codec and offer adaptive noise cancellation that uses Galaxy AI to filter unwanted noise.

  • The mophie powerstation pro XL is a portable power bank with a 25,000mAh capacity that delivers up to 100W of fast charging power via three USB-C PD (Power Delivery) ports, allowing you to charge a laptop and other devices simultaneously. It provides multiple hours of extra battery life for phones, tablets, and laptops and features a stylish, durable fabric finish. An LED indicator displays the remaining battery life and charging status.

  • This 1-metre charge cable is made with a woven design — with USB-C connectors on both ends — and is ideal for charging, syncing and transferring data between USB-C devices. It supports charging of up to 60 watts and transfers data at USB 2 rates.
